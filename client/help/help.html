Float Retriever
This is called a bookmarklet. When you are on a steamcommunity market skin page or Bitskins search page, click the bookmark. It will create buttons under each item. For SCM, once you have loaded the float with the CSGOfloat browser extension, you can click the new buttons which will add it to a list. For bitskins, you can click the buttons to add floats to list immediately. Check the console (f12) and paste the list into Float Creator when you are ready.
